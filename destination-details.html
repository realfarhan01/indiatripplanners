Heritage South India Tour

  • Home
  • Heritage South India Tour

Information

Commence on an enriching 15-day Heritage & Cultural Tour South India Tour Package with Driver India Private Tours and delve deep into the captivating cultural and historical treasures of this diverse region. This carefully crafted itinerary will take you on an unforgettable journey through the cultural heartland of South India, showcasing the rich heritage, architectural wonders, and traditional splendor that define this enchanting part of the country.

Your adventure begins in the vibrant city of Chennai, where a mix of ancient temples, colonial architecture, and modern developments awaits. Explore iconic landmarks such as the majestic Marina Beach, Fort St. George, and the colorful Kapaleeshwarar Temple. As you wander through the bustling streets of Chennai, you'll get a taste of the city's unique blend of tradition and modernity. Next, head to the charming town of Pondicherry, renowned for its French Quarter and serene Aurobindo Ashram. The distinct French influence is evident in the architecture and cuisine of this coastal town, creating an atmosphere of tranquil allure. Take a leisurely stroll along the promenade, indulge in delicious French pastries, and find moments of inner peace at the Aurobindo Ashram. Continue your journey to the historical city of Thanjavur, a UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its ancient Brihadeeswarar Temple. Admire the intricate architecture and richly adorned sculptures that pay tribute to the artistic brilliance of the Chola dynasty. Explore the beautiful Saraswati Mahal Library, housing an impressive collection of ancient manuscripts.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Chennai, Transfer to the Hotel and Explore.

Upon your arrival at Chennai's international airport, our representative will warmly welcome you. After a brief introduction, you will be transferred to your hotel to relax and freshen up after your journey. Depending on your arrival time, you can explore the local markets or relax at the hotel.

Day 2: Chennai Sightseeing Tour.

After breakfast, embark on a sightseeing tour of Chennai, the vibrant capital of Tamil Nadu. Visit Marina Beach, the second-longest urban beach in the world, and Fort St. George, the first British fortress in India. Explore Kapaleeshwarar Temple, a magnificent Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. In the afternoon, head towards Pondicherry.

Day 3: Chennai to Pondicherry en-route visit Mahabalipuram.

Today, after breakfast, check out from your Chennai hotel and proceed to Pondicherry. En route, you will visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Mahabalipuram. Explore the ancient rock-cut temples and sculptures, including the majestic Shore Temple, the breathtaking Arjuna's Penance, and the Five Rathas, each carved from a single rock. Admire the intricate stone carvings that depict fascinating mythological stories. Afterward, continue your journey to Pondicherry, a city known for its unique blend of French and Indian culture.

Day 4: Pondicherry Sightseeing Tour.

Discover the charm of Pondicherry as you explore its distinct French Quarter with its colonial-era architecture and tree-lined boulevards. Stroll along the picturesque Promenade Beach and soak in the tranquil atmosphere. Visit the renowned Aurobindo Ashram, a spiritual community founded by Sri Aurobindo and the Mother, where you can experience serenity and meditation. In the afternoon, explore Auroville, an experimental township that focuses on spiritual harmony and sustainable living.

Day 5: Pondicherry to Thanjavur en-route visit Chidambaram.

Today, embark on a scenic drive to Thanjavur, a city renowned for its cultural and artistic heritage. En route, visit Chidambaram, known for the ancient Nataraja Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva as the cosmic dancer. Marvel at the intricate sculptures and the vibrant atmosphere of the temple. Continue your journey to Thanjavur, also known as the "Rice Bowl of Tamil Nadu."

Day 6: Thanjavur Sightseeing Tour.

Explore the highlights of Thanjavur, including the Brihadeeswarar Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the largest temples in India. Admire the magnificent architecture and the intricate carvings that showcase the architectural brilliance of the Chola dynasty. Visit the Thanjavur Royal Palace and Art Gallery to learn more about the rich history and culture of the region.

Day 7: Drive from Thanjavur to Chettinad.

Drive to Chettinad, a region renowned for its unique architecture, cultural heritage, and flavorful cuisine. Chettinad is famous for its grand mansions, known as Chettinad Mansions, characterized by their opulence and intricate craftsmanship. Enjoy a traditional Chettinad meal, known for its aromatic spices and bold flavors.

Day 8: Chettinad Sightseeing Tour.

Immerse yourself in the rural charm of Chettinad as you explore the villages and interact with the locals to learn about their traditional way of life. Visit the famous Athangudi tile factories, known for their vibrant and handmade tiles. Explore the Chettinad Palace and other historic sites to gain insights into the region's rich cultural heritage.

Day 9: Driving from Chettinad to Madurai.

Travel to Madurai, one of the oldest cities in India and a center of Tamil culture and spirituality. In the evening, visit the iconic Meenakshi Amman Temple, a splendid example of Dravidian architecture, and witness the evening prayer ceremony, a mesmerizing experience.

Day 10: Madurai Sightseeing Tour.

Spend the day exploring the cultural landmarks of Madurai. Visit the Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace, known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture and majestic domes. Explore the Gandhi Memorial Museum, which showcases the life and teachings of Mahatma Gandhi. Visit the Vandiyur Mariamman Teppakulam, a massive temple tank, and enjoy the serene surroundings.

Day 11: Travel from Madurai to Thekkady.

Travel to Thekkady, located in the Western Ghats and famous for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Enjoy a boat safari on Periyar Lake, where you might spot elephants, deer, and various bird species in their natural habitat. Thekkady is also known for its spice plantations, so you can take a spice plantation tour to learn about the cultivation and processing of various spices.

Day 12: Thekkady Wildlife Activities.

Engage in nature-based activities like a jungle trek or bamboo rafting to immerse yourself in the rich biodiversity of Thekkady. You can also opt for an adventurous wildlife safari in the Periyar Tiger Reserve to spot elusive tigers and other wildlife.
